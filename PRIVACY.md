# Privacy Policy for Pomodo: Time Manager

**Last Updated:** March 2026

Pomodo is built with privacy and performance as its core principles. We believe your focus data belongs exclusively to you.

## 1. Zero Data Transmission
Pomodo operates entirely locally on your device. **We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your personal data, browser history, or usage statistics to any external servers.**

## 2. Local Storage
All your settings, custom timers, and productivity history are saved locally on your machine using Chrome's native `chrome.storage` API. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly choose to export it (e.g., via the CSV download feature).

## 3. Focus Shield (Website Blocking)
The Focus Shield feature uses the `declarativeNetRequest` API to block websites that you designate as distracting during focus sessions. The extension does not read, record, or track your browsing activity outside of evaluating whether the current URL matches your local blocklist.

## 4. Updates to this Policy
As Pomodo evolves, we may update this policy. Any changes will be reflected here, but our core philosophy—zero data harvesting—will never change.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in our feedback repository or contact the developer directly.
